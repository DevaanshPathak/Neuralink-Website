Neuralink

Neuralink Corp. is an American neurotechnology company that is developing implantable brain–computer interfaces (BCIs), based in Fremont, California, as of 2022. Founded by Elon Musk and a team of seven scientists and engineers, Neuralink was launched in 2016 and was first publicly reported in March 2017.

Implants

Neuralink's tiny implants, with thousands of threads recording and stimulating neurons, aim to bridge the brain and tech. They're currently restoring movement in paralyzed individuals and hold potential for treating disorders, enhancing abilities, and even enabling brain-to-brain communication. But like any powerful tech, safety, ethics, and scientific unknowns need careful consideration as we navigate this mind-blowing future.

Surgrical Robot

Elon Musk's Neuralink is developing a 7-foot-tall surgical robot that can implant wires into the brain. The robot has cameras, sensors, and a laser-milled needle that hooks into a loop at the end of each wire. The robot aims to avoid creating bleeding or scar tissue in the brain.
